The Forsaken Soldier

The Forsaken Soldier is a narrative-driven adventure game inspired by the Moriones Festival of the Philippines. Set in the island province of Marinduque, the game follows the story of Longinus, a Roman centurion cursed and cast out after witnessing a miracle during the crucifixion of Christ. As players explore vibrant festival streets, participate in traditional customs, and uncover hidden truths, they must help Longinus seek redemption while evading those who still hunt him.
